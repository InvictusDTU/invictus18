About Us

The Legacy : INVICTUS


Invictus is the first edition as the Umbrella Techfest , being organized jointly by all the technical societies of Delhi Technological University.

Carrying on the legacy of the technical fests at Delhi Technological University, this year's fest will again be a platform for budding technocrats to explore and put their talent to test when they get to compete with other prodigies of their field in the nation.

The techfest will cover a wide variety of events mainly in the technical domain with some other miscellaneous events.

The events range from as conventional as Quizzes and Fun games to as advanced as Aerial Robotics and Machine Learning. The whole fest will be divided into zones where you would find same genre events and exciting cash prizes would await. The fest would cater to all kind of technocrats with skills ranging from soft skills to coding skills, hardware prowess to simulation and designing wizards.
